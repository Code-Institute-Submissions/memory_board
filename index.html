Football Memory Game

Game Instructions
The aim of the game is to find pairs of cards in the fewest amount of flips and within the 90 second time limit.
To start just click on "Click to Start", this will start the clock. Then simply click on a card then click another card to try to find its pair. If the pair is not found then the cards will flip back, just try to remember where they were, if the cards do match they will remain face up.
The easiest level has 12 matching pairs to find before the time is up, the medium level has 16 pairs and the hard level has 20. Each level needs to be completed in 90 seconds.
Good Luck and Enjoy.
